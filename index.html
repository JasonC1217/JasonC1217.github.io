About Me

I'm a Master's student in Data Science at UC San Diego, with a background in Neurobiology and Cognitive Science. My research focuses on integrating machine learning with single-cell multiomics to uncover transcriptional and epigenetic changes in the brain due to HIV infection and opioid use disorder. I’m passionate about combining biology, statistics, and computational tools to understand complex neurological diseases and improve human health.
