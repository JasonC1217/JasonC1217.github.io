About Me

I'm a Data Scientist and incoming PhD student in Biomedical Sciences at Pennsylvania State University, currently completing my Master's in Data Science at UC San Diego with a background in Neurobiology and Cognitive Science. My research focuses on integrating machine learning with single-cell multiomics to uncover transcriptional and epigenetic changes in the brain due to HIV infection and opioid use disorder. I’m passionate about combining biology, statistics, and computational tools to understand complex neurological diseases and improve human health.
